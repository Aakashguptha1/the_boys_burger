OUR STORY

From a Small Grill to Big Bites How It All Began

Since 2021, we've been college students chasing education with empty pockets but full hearts. With no financial support, we worked jobs at 19 just to keep studying. One idea under a neem tree near Jamal Mohamed College changed everything. We started a small food stall — no cheers, just dreams. With borrowed utensils and big belief, we served our first 3 customers. Bit by bit, our stall became a student favorite and our story, a source of hope. We studied by night, cooked by day, and paid every fee ourselves. Today, we proudly run three food outlets born from that first spark. No handouts — just hustle, heart, and homemade flavors. This isn't just food. This is our journey — and it's just the beginning.
